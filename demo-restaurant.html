Our story

A neighbourhood
restaurant.

Marigold opened in 2019 with a simple brief: cook honest food with good ingredients, and build a room where people actually want to spend time. No concept, no gimmick.

Our menu changes with the seasons. We work with a small handful of suppliers we trust and try to waste as little as possible. V = vegetarian. VE = vegan.
